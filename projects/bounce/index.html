Bounce

Bounce is the lightweight front-end game slot: easy to ship, easy to link from the main site, and a bloody good example of a project that just wants static assets and a tiny deploy surface. Physics-powered. Satisfaction guaranteed or your money back (it's free, so...)

Why it exists

The original browser game experiment stays visible as the playful north star: a small, tactile prototype with room to get real weird. Watch that ball go. Bloody mesmerizing.

Main site stays easy to publish, while project services can live on shared infrastructure with Traefik, cloudflared, and one Postgres instance carved into neat little schemas.

Fun fact: The ball has a mind of its own. We tried to train it. Failed.
